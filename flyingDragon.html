Flying Dragon Facts

  • They can glide more than 50 feet
  • They can lay up to 5 eggs
  • They are also known as Draco (Latin for dragon) lizards
  • They are extremely territorial and will glide after intruders
  • The "wings" they use to gilde are called patagia
