Chinese Water Dragon Facts

  • They can stay underwater for up to 25 minutes
  • They communitcate via head bobbing, arm waving, and throat inflation
  • They are ocean drifters, meaning they let the wind and currents carry them
  • They only grow up to 3cm in length
  • Their lifespan is between one month and one year
